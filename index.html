About

I am an architectural designer and researcher interested in how data, infrastructure, and social dynamics shape contemporary cities. My work moves between scales—from territorial analysis to building-level strategies—always asking how design can redistribute access, visibility, and opportunity.

Across projects, I collaborate with civic data, mobility systems, and housing policies to imagine alternative futures for public space, domesticity, and collective life.
